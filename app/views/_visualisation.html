About

The page visualizes the current state of the humanities as well as one of its possible futures. The words on the left side of the page represent the 11 concepts that are were most tweeted about in combination with the hashtag humanities on the day that this site went live. The words on the right side are the ones that we want you to tweet about. The gray boxes indicate whether or not a concept is currently is in the top 20 of most discussed humanities concepts. Whenever one of the concepts on this page is tweeted about, its square will light up in red.
