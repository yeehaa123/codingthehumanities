This page visualizes the state of the humanities and its possible futures

The words on the left side represent the 11 concepts that were most tweeted about in combination with #humanities on the day that this site went live (September 17th, 2013). The words on the right side are the ones that we want you to tweet about. The gray boxes indicate that a concept is currently is in the top 20 of most discussed humanities concepts. Whenever one of the concepts on this page is tweeted about, its square will instantly light up in red.
